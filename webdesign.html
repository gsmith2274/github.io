Our Approach

Every website we build starts from a blank file — not a template, not a drag-and-drop builder, not WordPress. We write clean, semantic HTML and CSS by hand. That means faster load times, better search engine visibility, and code you actually own and can take anywhere.

We believe a website should be a long-term asset, not a recurring liability. Our builds are straightforward to maintain, easy to update, and don't depend on third-party subscriptions staying active.

Our Process

  1. Discovery — A short call to understand your business, audience, goals, and timeline.
  2. Proposal — We send a written scope, page list, timeline, and flat-rate price. No hourly surprises.
  3. Design — We build a visual mockup and share it for your feedback before writing a single line of code.
  4. Development — We code the full site, mobile-first, with accessibility and performance built in.
  5. Review — You get a live preview link. We make revisions based on your feedback.
  6. Launch — We deploy to your domain, verify everything, and hand over all files.
  7. Support — Post-launch support window included with every package.

Design Principles

  • Mobile-first — Every site is designed for phones before desktops.
  • Semantic HTML — Proper heading structure, landmark elements, and alt text on every image.
  • Fast by default — No unnecessary scripts, no bloated frameworks, no third-party trackers unless you ask for them.
  • Accessible — Keyboard navigable, sufficient color contrast, screen reader friendly.
  • You own it — All source files delivered at launch. No licence fees, no platform dependency.

Common Questions

Can I edit the site myself after it's built?

Yes. We write plain HTML files — you can open them in any text editor and make changes. We're also happy to show you how during the handoff call.

Do you use WordPress?

We don't, by default. WordPress is powerful but adds complexity, maintenance overhead, and security exposure that most small business sites don't need. If you specifically need WordPress, ask us — we can discuss it.

What if I already have a logo and brand colors?

Great — send them over. We'll match your existing brand. If you don't have a logo yet, we offer logo design as an add-on.

Will my site show up on Google?

Yes. We include basic on-page SEO in every build — proper titles, meta descriptions, heading structure, and a sitemap. For a full SEO audit and optimization, see our add-on services.
